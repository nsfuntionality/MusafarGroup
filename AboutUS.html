About US

Musafar Group LLC was established in 2020 to meet the need for a personalized, low-cost, quality alternative to the expedited customer that were currently available. 

Akal Trucking LLC is a Wisconsin based, owned and operated company. We are committed to providing the personalized service you require on your critical shipments at a competitive rate.

  • 2011-2012

    Our Humble Beginnings

    We Started with our partner company Akal Trucking LLC in 2011. we have started with 1 truck journey hauling loads and parking at truck stops. 

  • March 2019

    An Company is Born

    After getting Akal Trucking LLC and partnering with other partners, to follow One Stop That's All moto for our truck buddies. we decided to get bigger.

  • December 2019

    Phase 1 Construction

    We got the city approval and started our construction for Phase 1. Which will have about 30 Truck parking spots, 2 Bay for Repair Shop and Huge Office space for our Partners Dispatching.

  • July 2021

    Phase 2 Expansion

    Phase Two will extend our Parking spots form 30 to 200 and along with 2 Bays, we will have 4 more bays. and Distribution center.
